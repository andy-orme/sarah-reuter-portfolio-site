About me

Sarah Reuter is a freelance photographer and writer working in the art and music industries.

With an early interest in documentary photography, she went on to study at Falmouth University where she developed an interest in curation. Her practice examines themes of institutional and societal critique, particularly focusing her thoughts on the role of photography in our daily lives.

As well as curating numerous exhibitions across the country and developing her own photographic practice, Sarah has worked as a freelance photographer with a specialism in photographing art installations and creating album artwork for musicians.

A passionate advocate for the use of photography as a therapeutic tool, she has devised her own series of workshops and campaigns to raise the profile of female photographers in an otherwise patriarchal platform.

Currently working in the world of music, Sarah is as a freelance writer for popular contemporary music blog Audiotox under her pseudonym Violet.
