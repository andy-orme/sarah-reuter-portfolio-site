Fripps & Fripps - Pest Control

Consider this your midweek injection of energy because these Sydney-based rockers have lined up quite the hit!

‘Pest Control’ is the latest track to be released from their second EP due out later this year. Straight off the bat this track soars straight in with a simmering beat and raucous guitars, soon to be joined by the slickest of vocals, spitting lyrics in a convergence of rock and hip-hop styles.

With a distinct Aussi-indie sound and hints of British rock legends such as The Arctic Monkeys and a personal fave, The Kooks, we are seriously digging these guy’s vibes.
