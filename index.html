Hi, I’m Sarah & I do

Sarah Reuter is a freelance photographer and writer working in the art and music industries. With an early interest in documentary photography, she went on to study at Falmouth University where she developed an interest in curation and explores the use of the camera phone as a contemporary photographic tool. Currently working in the world of music, Sarah is as a freelance writer for popular contemporary music blog Audiotox under her pseudonym UltraViolet.
